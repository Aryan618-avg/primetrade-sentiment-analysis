Key Insights & Findings

🏆

Extreme Greed = Highest Win Rate (89.2%)

Traders perform best when the market is in Extreme Greed — likely due to trend momentum. However, average PnL is also highest here ($130/trade), suggesting professionals capitalize on bull runs efficiently.

📉

Fear is the Best Time to BUY

BUY trades executed during Fear periods averaged $209.65 — nearly 4× the profit of BUY trades in Extreme Greed ($28.98). This is classic contrarian trading: buy when others are fearful.

📈

Extreme Greed = Best Time to SELL

SELL trades in Extreme Greed averaged $176.05, dramatically outperforming Extreme Fear sells ($50.26). Smart traders take profit at sentiment peaks — a clear market-top signal.

⚡

One Trader Has a 99.1% Win Rate

Account 0xbaaaf657... executed 9,997 trades with a near-perfect 99.1% win rate earning $940K. This statistical outlier likely employs market-making or delta-neutral strategies.

⚠️

Aug 2024 Was the Worst Month

August 2024 saw the only significant losing month (−$107K), coinciding with a Fear/Greed score drop to ~33 (Fear zone). This validates sentiment as a risk signal for drawdown periods.

🔗

Weak but Noteworthy Correlation

Daily PnL vs sentiment correlation = −0.098. Counterintuitively negative — higher greed days don't directly produce higher profits, because profitable contrarian traders go against the trend.
